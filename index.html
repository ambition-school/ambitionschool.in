About Our School

Government of Odisha Recognized Educational Institution

Our school provides education from Class 4th to Class 10th. It is a C.O.R certified and Government of Odisha recognized educational institution in Nayagarh District.

Our school's goal is to shape students with education, culture, discipline, and self-reliance. Modern teaching methods are employed under the guidance of experienced and qualified teachers.
